Oatmeal

Description

This is Annelise's go-to meal, snack or treat. It's a simple recipe that's easy to get wrong, so pay close attention.

Ingredients

Yield: One serving

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats, mounded to be more than 1/2 cup
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon Splenda sweetener, where the 1/2 tablespoon is not measured precisely, but by vibes
  • 1 hearty dash of table salt
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Add oats to a ceramic bowl.
  2. Add sweetener and salt to the bowl, do not mix.
  3. Pour in water.
  4. Place in microwave for two minutes, thirty seconds.
